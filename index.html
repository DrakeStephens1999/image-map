2019 Melee Rankings

An Image Map

Why This Photo?

Super Smash Brothers Melee for the Nintendo GameCube, this game shaped my entire highschool mind. Because of this I have friends, seen the world and received a healthy outlet for competition. For all that and more I was happy to choose something so special to me to code around. This picture illustrates the top 9 players of 2019. Unfortunately these are the most recent rankings, because as it did for everyone covid got in the way.

The three sections of my map that I chose are as follows: Player’s names, the character that they play, and the ranking movements from the previous year (red and green column). The player’s names are obviously important because they worked hard for their names to be on the list. The characters are here in case the viewer wants to know more about the characters and their advanced techniques. Finally the rankings, I put them here in case the viewer wanted to see where the players were positioned from the year prior.

While anyone visiting this page might not see my passion from the photo. Hopefully my words can get you excited to interact with it. One of my favorite facts of this photo is that IBDW moved 25 spots up in one year, that alone shows enough storytelling for any average competition buff to have their interest peaked. Or half of the characters shown here are foxes, making the outliers that much more exciting. When I heard I was going to be able to make a image map out of something that interests me. My answer was clear.

List of clickable areas on the image map

  • Players: Player's names are clickable and when clicked will send you to their wikepedia pages
  • Characters: All the little character icons will send you to their respective smashboards pages
  • The last column: The final column on the right is a list of how much each player moved in the last year. CLicking this link will send you to past year's rankings
